# App Listing for Google Play Store

## App Name:
Xerivo To-Do

## Short Description (up to 80 characters):
Plan clearly, track progress, and finish with focus.

## Full Description (up to 4000 characters):

Meet Xerivo To-Do, your new essential partner for personal productivity. Designed for clarity and efficiency, Xerivo helps you capture, organize, and complete tasks without the clutter. Whether you're planning your day, managing a project, or simply trying to build better habits, our intuitive, local-first app ensures your data is always fast, private, and available.

**Key Features:**

*   **Quick Capture:** Instantly add tasks and notes as they come to mind. Our streamlined interface lets you capture details like due dates, priority, and categories without breaking your flow.
*   **Smart Dashboard:** Get a clear overview of your day. The main dashboard shows your progress at a glance, with a clean breakdown of total, active, and completed tasks, plus a progress bar to keep you motivated.
*   **Flexible Organization:** Structure your workflow your way. Assign tasks to customizable categories like "Deep Work," "Design," or "Health." Set priorities (High, Medium, Low) and due dates (Today, Tomorrow, +7d) to stay on top of what matters most.
*   **Recurring Tasks:** Build habits with ease. Set tasks to repeat daily or weekly, and Xerivo will automatically create the next one for you upon completion.
*   **Powerful Filtering:** Quickly find what you're looking for. Filter your list to view all tasks, just active ones, or those you've already completed.
*   **Clean & Focused Design:** Enjoy a beautiful, minimalist interface that puts your tasks front and center. Our focus is on helping you get things done, not on distracting you.

Download Xerivo To-Do today and start planning, tracking, and finishing with focus!
