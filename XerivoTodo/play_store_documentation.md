# Xerivo To-Do: Plan, Track, and Achieve Your Goals

Xerivo To-Do is a simple and intuitive to-do list app that helps you stay organized and focused. With Xerivo To-Do, you can easily plan your tasks, track your progress, and achieve your goals.

## How to Use

### 1. Add a new task
To add a new task, simply type the task description in the "Task" field and tap the "Add Task" button.

![Main screen](Screenshot%20of%20Medium%20Phone%20API%2036.1.png)

### 2. Add details to your task
You can add more details to your task, such as notes, due date, priority, and category.

![Adding a task](Screenshot%20of%20Medium%20Phone%20API%2036.2.png)

### 3. Track your progress
Once you've added a few tasks, you can track your progress on the main screen. The progress bar shows you how much you've completed, and the list of tasks shows you what's left to do.

![Main screen with tasks](Screenshot%20of%20Medium%20Phone%20API%2036.3.png)
